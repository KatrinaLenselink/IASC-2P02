The first Fitbit was released in October 2017. It is a similar design to the Apple watch, but it tracks heart rate using a technology called PurePulse, which was inspired by a technique called photoplethysmography. This term means using light to measure blood flow. The PurePulse technology shines a green light onto wrist and the light detectors would measure the amount light absorbed. This shows how fast your heart beats per minute. The Fitbit uses this technology to measure resting heart rate and cardio fitness levels. The Fitbit tracks heart rate data from workouts to improve heart rate for further exercising.
